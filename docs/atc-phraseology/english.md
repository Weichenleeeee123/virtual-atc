# ICAO Standard ATC Phraseology

## Departure Phase

### Pilot Requests
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, stand XX, request startup"
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, request pushback"
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, request taxi"
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, runway XX, ready for departure"

### ATC Responses
- "XX Air XXX, XX Tower, startup approved"
- "XX Air XXX, pushback approved, face XX"
- "XX Air XXX, taxi to runway XX via taxiway XX, wind XX at XX"
- "XX Air XXX, runway XX, cleared for takeoff, wind XX at XX"
- "XX Air XXX, standby"
- "XX Air XXX, hold position, traffic landing"

## En-route Phase

### Pilot Requests
- "XX Approach, XX Air XXX, flight level XXX, request approach"
- "XX Approach, XX Air XXX, request descent"

### ATC Responses
- "XX Air XXX, XX Approach, radar contact, maintain flight level XXX"
- "XX Air XXX, descend and maintain flight level XXX"
- "XX Air XXX, turn left heading XXX"
- "XX Air XXX, turn right heading XXX"
- "XX Air XXX, reduce speed to XXX knots"

## Arrival Phase

### Pilot Requests
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, final approach, request landing"
- "XX Tower, XX Air XXX, going around"

### ATC Responses
- "XX Air XXX, runway XX, cleared to land, wind XX at XX"
- "XX Air XXX, continue approach"
- "XX Air XXX, go around, climb to XXX feet"
- "XX Air XXX, vacate runway, contact ground XXX.X"

## Frequency Changes

### ATC Instructions
- "XX Air XXX, contact XX Approach XXX.X"
- "XX Air XXX, contact XX Tower XXX.X"
- "XX Air XXX, contact Ground XXX.X"

### Pilot Readback
- "Contact XX Approach XXX.X, XX Air XXX, good day"
- "Contact XX Tower XXX.X, XX Air XXX"

## Standard Responses

### Acknowledgments
- "Roger" (Message received)
- "Wilco" (Will comply)
- "Say again" (Repeat)
- "Standby" (Wait)
- "Affirm" (Yes)
- "Negative" (No)

### Altitude Confirmations
- "Maintain flight level XXX, XX Air XXX"
- "Descending to flight level XXX, XX Air XXX"
- "Climbing to flight level XXX, XX Air XXX"

### Heading Confirmations
- "Turning left heading XXX, XX Air XXX"
- "Turning right heading XXX, XX Air XXX"

## Emergency Situations

- "XX Air XXX, declaring emergency"
- "XX Air XXX, request priority landing"
- "XX Air XXX, minimum fuel"
- "Mayday, Mayday, Mayday, XX Air XXX"
- "Pan-Pan, Pan-Pan, Pan-Pan, XX Air XXX"
